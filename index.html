ingenoire's PSO2 Symbol Arts

I ported these Symbol Arts from the the world of imagery into the world of shapes, and even offer you the source file for easier editing using this online symbol art editor.

To install, simply drop the .SAR file into your Documents\SEGA\PHANTASYSTARONLINE2_NA_STEAM\symbolarts\import, then open PSO2, go to Symbol Arts, set the current tab to Import, and press Import. Your SA should appear in it. Press save and override a slot of your SA palette, and you should be able to use it.

If you need to contact me regarding these SA, contact me on twitter @ingenoire.
